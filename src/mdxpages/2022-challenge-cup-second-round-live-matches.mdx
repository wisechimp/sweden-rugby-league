It's the second round of the Challenge Cup this weekend and several matches will be freely available to stream online on Saturday and Sunday.

The Sportsman will be showing coverage of the clash between the Army and the Navy through their YouTube channel on Saturday, the <OutboundLink href="https://www.thesportsman.com/articles/army-vs-royal-navy-2022-betfred-challenge-cup-round-2" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">first time</OutboundLink> any of the British Armed Forces have met in the competition.

The following day Rochdale Hornets, celebrating the centenary of their 1922 Challenge Cup win, will face the recently rebranded Midlands Hurricanes in a game that will be broadcast on the BBC's website this Sunday.

Lastly Oldham will be bringing the round to a close with their match against Lock Lane, available through the RFL's "Our League" app (registration required).

Times shown are local kick off times in the UK:

- Saturday 2pm British Army v Royal Navy (<OutboundLink href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uAIqOTx9J2U" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Live</OutboundLink> on The Sportsman's YouTube channel)
- Sunday 1pm Rochdale Hornets v Midlands Hurricanes (Live on BBC Sport website)
- Sunday 6pm Oldham v Lock Lane (Live on the "Our League" App - [free registration](https://www.rugby-league.com/ourleague/membership) required)
